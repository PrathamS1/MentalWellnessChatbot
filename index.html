Tips If You Are Dealing With These

STRESS
ANXIETY
DEPRESSION

STRESS

  • Get more physical activity
  • Follow a healthy diet
  • Minimize phone use and screen time
  • Practice self-care
  • Spend time with friends and family
  • Create boundaries and learn to say no
  • Learn to avoid procrastination

ANXIETY

  • Keep physically active.
  • Use stress management and relaxation techniques.
  • Learn about your disorder.
  • Make sleep a priority.
  • Stick to your treatment plan.
  • Keep a journal.
  • Identify triggers.

DEPRESSION

  • Socializing can improve your mood.
  • Take up some form of exercise.
  • Don't avoid the things you find difficult.
  • Try to eat a healthy diet.
  • Do things that make you feel good.
  • Develop a “wellness toolbox” to deal with depression.
  • Challenge negative thinking.
